Learning Statement

“I want to be great or nothing,”
 - Amy March

Alanis Morisette sings, “swallow it down, that jagged little pill,” off her song You Learn. If I had to categorize my undergraduate experience, it would be that entire album, all based around that one line. My life is centered around music. When I have hard days, or good days, or any days, I end up back at there. Listening to it, creating it, experiencing it. Understanding my college years as albums can help paint the experiences I went through as a Husky from 2020-2024.
